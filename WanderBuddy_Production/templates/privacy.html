Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 11, 2026

1. Introduction

Travel & Trouble ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our website and services.

2. Information We Collect

Personal Information:
  • Name, email address, phone number
  • Billing and payment information
  • Emergency contact details
  • Government ID (for booking verification)
Travel Information:
  • Booking history and preferences
  • Travel dates and destinations
  • Dietary requirements and medical conditions
  • Activity preferences
Technical Information:
  • IP address and browser type
  • Device information
  • Usage data and cookies

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Process your bookings and payments
  • Communicate trip details and updates
  • Ensure your safety during trips
  • Improve our services and user experience
  • Send promotional offers (with your consent)
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Data Sharing

We share your information only with:

  • Local Partners: Verified guides and accommodation providers necessary for your trip
  • Payment Processors: Secure payment gateways for transactions
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law or for safety purposes

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • SSL encryption for data transmission
  • Secure password hashing (PBKDF2)
  • Regular security audits
  • Limited access to personal data

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request corrections to your information
  • Request deletion of your account
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Download your data
