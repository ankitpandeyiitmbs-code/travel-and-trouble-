About Travel & Trouble

Our Story

Founded in 2026, Travel & Trouble was born from a simple idea: travel should be more than just visiting places – it should be about creating connections, experiencing local culture, and building a community of like-minded adventurers.

What started as a small group of college friends organizing weekend treks has grown into India's most loved adventure travel platform. We've taken over 10,000 travelers to explore the hidden gems of Himachal Pradesh, Uttarakhand, and beyond.

Our Mission

To make adventure travel accessible, affordable, and community-driven. We believe that everyone deserves to experience the magic of the Himalayas, the thrill of paragliding, and the serenity of mountain sunrises.
